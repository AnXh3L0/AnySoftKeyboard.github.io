AnySoftKeyboard

Android (f/w 1.5+) on-screen keyboard for multiple languages.

Features

  • Supports lots of languages via external packages. E.g., English (QWERTY, DVORAK, AZERTY and Colemak), Hebrew, Russian, Arabic, Lao, Bulgarian, Swiss, German, Swedish, Spanish, Catalan, Belorussian, Portuguese, Ukrainian and many more.
  • Physical keyboards are supported as well.
  • Theme (skin) support.
  • Auto capitalization.
  • Word suggestions, and Next-Word suggestions
  • Voice input.
  • Compact modes: Split and Compact to left/right.
  • Special keyboard for text fields which require only numbers, dates, email or URI addresses.
  • And many more features.
